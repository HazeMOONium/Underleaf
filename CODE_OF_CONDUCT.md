# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct — version 2.1

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others’ private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, wiki edits, or other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons when appropriate.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project. Examples of representing the project include using an official project email address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Reporting Guidelines
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **conduct@yourdomain.example**. All reports will be handled with discretion and with respect for the reporter’s privacy.

When reporting an incident, please include:
- A description of the incident with dates, times, and locations (if applicable)
- Names of any involved parties (if known)
- Any relevant screenshots, logs, links, or other supporting information
- Whether you want to remain anonymous

Maintainers will follow up promptly and will strive to acknowledge receipt of reports within 72 hours.

## Enforcement Process
When a report is received, maintainers will:
1. Acknowledge receipt (within 72 hours).
2. Evaluate the report and, where necessary, contact involved parties for more information.
3. Take appropriate action, which may include a private warning, temporary ban, permanent ban, or escalation to platform administrators or law enforcement if the behavior is criminal.
Decisions are final and may be reviewed by an appointed committee of maintainers when appropriate.
